Around The World
Around The World - The Language Club Of Shiv Nadar University. The Primary Purpose Is To Allow Students To Broaden Their Knowledge Of Language And Culture From Across India As Well As Other Parts Of The World. To Better Understand SNU's Everchanging Environment And Communicate With Their Peers, We Believe That By Establishing A Space Where Individuals From All Over The Country Can Celebrate Their Cultures, Festivals, And Languages Via Entertaining Activities, We May Help Instill A Better Appreciation For People From All Over The World.
